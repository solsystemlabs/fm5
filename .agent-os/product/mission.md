# Product Mission

> Last Updated: 2025-08-10
> Version: 1.0.0

## Pitch
FilamentManager is a comprehensive 3D printing resource management platform that helps 3D printing business operators efficiently track filaments, models, products, and physical inventory by providing intelligent file parsing, automated resource tracking, and streamlined workflow management.

## Users
### Primary Customers
- 3D Printing Business Operators: Small to medium-sized 3D printing businesses that manage multiple projects, materials, and inventory items daily

### User Personas
**3D Printing Business Owner** (25-45 years)
- **Role:** Operations Manager/Owner
- **Context:** Manages a 3D printing service business with multiple printers, various filament types, and numerous client projects
- **Pain Points:** Difficulty tracking filament usage across projects, losing track of model variations and print settings, manual inventory management leading to material shortages
- **Goals:** Streamline operations, reduce material waste, improve project profitability through better resource tracking

## The Problem
### Resource Management Chaos
3D printing businesses struggle with tracking the complex relationships between filaments, models, print settings, and physical inventory. Operators often lose track of which filament colors and types are available, which model variations have been tested, and how much material is consumed per project. This leads to material waste, project delays, and reduced profitability.

**Our Solution:** Automated resource tracking with intelligent file parsing that extracts print settings, material usage, and duration from .3mf and .gcode files, creating a comprehensive database of all printing resources.

## Differentiators
### Intelligent File Parsing
Unlike generic inventory management tools, we provide specialized .3mf and .gcode file parsing that automatically extracts print duration, filament requirements, and material usage data. This results in zero-effort data entry for key project metrics and accurate material consumption tracking.

### 3D Printing-Specific Workflow
Unlike general inventory systems, we understand the unique relationships between filaments, models, print settings, and finished products in 3D printing operations. This results in streamlined workflows tailored specifically for additive manufacturing businesses.

### Integrated Model and Material Management
Unlike file storage solutions that only handle uploads/downloads, we provide integrated tracking of both digital assets (models) and physical resources (filaments, inventory). This results in complete visibility across the entire production pipeline.

## Key Features
### Core Features
- **Filament Management:** Track filament types, colors, quantities, and usage across all projects with automatic depletion calculations
- **Model Library:** Upload, organize, and version control .3mf and .gcode files with automatic metadata extraction
- **Intelligent File Parsing:** Automatically extract print duration, material requirements, and settings from uploaded model files
- **Inventory Tracking:** Monitor physical printed products and raw materials with low-stock alerts and reorder notifications
- **Search and Discovery:** Quickly find available filaments, models, and products using advanced filtering and search capabilities

### Collaboration Features
- **Project History:** Maintain detailed records of which filaments were used for specific models and their print outcomes
- **Resource Planning:** View material requirements and availability before starting new projects to prevent delays
- **Print Analytics:** Track material consumption, print success rates, and cost analysis across all projects
- **Export and Backup:** Download model files and export resource data for external use or backup purposes
- **Multi-User Access:** Enable team members to access and update resource information with role-based permissions
