# Product Mission (Lite)

Filament Manager (FM5) is a comprehensive 3D printing business management platform that helps small business owners manage their model files, physical inventory, and business operations by providing integrated file processing, inventory tracking, and expense management tools.

FM5 serves 3D printing small business owners who need to organize hundreds of model files, track physical inventory of printed products and filaments, and manage business expenses including vendor market participation. Unlike generic file managers or separate inventory tools, FM5 automatically extracts ZIP archives and 3MF metadata while connecting digital models directly to physical products and business operations in a unified platform.
