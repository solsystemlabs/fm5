# Product Mission

## Pitch

FileMaker 5 (FM5) is a comprehensive 3D printing business management platform that helps small business owners manage their model files, physical inventory, and business operations by providing integrated file processing, inventory tracking, and expense management tools.

## Users

### Primary Customers

- **3D Printing Small Business Owners**: Entrepreneurs running small-scale 3D printing operations who need to manage digital assets, physical products, and business operations
- **Maker Entrepreneurs**: Creative professionals selling custom 3D printed products who struggle with file organization and inventory management

### User Personas

**Sarah - Small Business Owner** (28-45 years old)

- **Role:** 3D Printing Business Owner
- **Context:** Runs a small business selling custom 3D printed items at maker fairs and online marketplaces
- **Pain Points:** Files scattered across folders, difficulty tracking which models have been printed, manual inventory counting, poor expense tracking
- **Goals:** Streamline file management, automate inventory tracking, improve business profitability through better expense management

## The Problem

### File Management Chaos

3D printing businesses accumulate hundreds of model files, variations, and associated images with no systematic organization. Business owners waste hours searching for files and lose track of which models have been successful products.

**Our Solution:** Automated file extraction and organization with intelligent categorization and search capabilities.

### Inventory Blind Spots

Small business owners struggle to track physical inventory of printed products and filament supplies, leading to stockouts during sales events and overordering of slow-moving items.

**Our Solution:** Integrated inventory management that connects digital models to physical products with real-time stock levels.

### Business Expense Tracking

3D printing businesses have complex expense structures including filament costs, model licensing, hardware purchases, and event participation fees that are difficult to track manually.

**Our Solution:** Comprehensive expense tracking integrated with inventory and sales event management for complete business visibility.

## Differentiators

### Automated File Extraction

Unlike generic file managers, FM5 automatically extracts ZIP archives containing 3MF and STL files, organizing existing images and extracting metadata already present in 3MF files. This eliminates manual file organization and provides instant access to model information.

### Product-Centric Workflow

Unlike traditional inventory systems, FM5 connects digital models directly to physical products and filament usage, providing end-to-end traceability from digital file to sold product.

### Integrated Business Management

Unlike separate tools for files, inventory, and expenses, FM5 provides a unified platform that connects all aspects of a 3D printing business, enabling data-driven decisions about which models to print and which events to attend.

## Key Features

### Core Features

- **Model Management:** Add, edit, and organize 3D model files with automatic metadata extraction from 3MF files
- **Automatic File Processing:** Extract ZIP files containing 3MF/STL files and organize existing images
- **Cloud Storage Integration:** Store files securely in S3 with fast download capabilities
- **Model Variations:** Track different sliced versions of models with specific print settings
- **Product Management:** Connect model variations to physical products with pricing and descriptions

### Inventory Features

- **Physical Product Tracking:** Monitor stock levels of printed products tied to specific model variations
- **Filament Management:** Track filament inventory with automatic usage calculations
- **Low Stock Alerts:** Receive notifications when inventory falls below defined thresholds

### Business Management Features

- **Expense Tracking:** Record and categorize business expenses including filament purchases and hardware costs
- **Sales Event Management:** Plan and track vendor markets with scheduling, costs, locations, and mileage
- **Receipt Management:** Digital storage and organization of business receipts and documentation
- **AI Event Discovery:** Optional AI integration to help discover local maker fairs, craft shows, and vendor markets in the user's area
